# Integrations

Integrating Remedy with your team’s communication platform enhances collaboration and productivity by seamlessly connecting your workflow to your communication hub.&#x20;

This integration allows you to receive real-time notifications, share updates, and manage tasks directly within your existing communication tools, ensuring your team stays aligned and informed without switching between multiple platforms.
